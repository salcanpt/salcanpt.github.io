Technology Training

We provide technology training services to end-users at all levels of the organisation including operators, maintenance personnel, engineers and management. Training services typically include:

  • Production of Operations and Maintenance Manuals for plant equipment and systems
  • "Hands on" Classroom based training with off-line simulation systems
  • Training curriculum and course development

Time and time again we come across projects that fail for the simple reason that the end user has not been adequately trained. We believe that end-user training is one of the most important project deliverables. You can install and commission a superbly engineered system but if you do not train and get buy-in from the end user then the project is on a very slippery slope.
