Coal Mine De-gassing and CSG wellhead skids

We  have recently been involved in delivering a coal mine degassing service for a Queensland based client. The client wishes to begin underground mining of the coal in the near future, but wants to first reduce the methane gas concentration in the coal deposits prior to commencing mine development. 

Our scope included:

  • Design of well head skids and flare skids including process, mechanical, instrumentation, electrical and controls design
  • Project Management of skid and electrical panel fabrication
  • Production of Hazardous Area Verification Dossier
  • Specification of gas flares, generators and associated controls integration
  • Implementation of gas field SCADA system and well head controller
  • Commissioning and operational management of gas wells, flares and generators
  • On-going operational support
  • SCADA system hosting
