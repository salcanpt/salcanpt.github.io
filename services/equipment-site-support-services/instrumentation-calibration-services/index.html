Instrumentation & Calibration Services

SALCAN Process Technology has experience specifying and configuring field instrumentation for various applications and industries. We have worked with conventional, HART and bus based instruments across multiple vendors.

We also specialise in the development and optimisation of plant calibration systems ensuring that the readings you receive from your instrument are both accurate and precise. Read our views on instrument calibration here and why it iss important that your organisation is calibrating for the right reasons.

SALCAN Process Technology has detailed experience in setting up / optimising calibration programs in addition to provision of external auditing capabilities. To discuss any specific element(s) of a calibration program please do not hesitate to contact us.
