Our business provides solutions that deliver the lowest total cost of ownership to our clients.

Welcome to SALCAN Process Technology. SALCAN is an Industrial Process Control & Automation consultancy as well as a licensed Electrical Contractor.

SALCAN Process Technology offer:

  • Engineering design services for industrial electrical, instrumentation and control system solutions. 
  • Stand-alone power system design for remote applications.
  • Telemetry solutions for data acquisition and control via a variety of LTE, data radio and satellite networks.
  • Hazardous Area classification and design services to AS/NZS60079.
  • Hazardous Area equipment inspection and maintenance services.
  • Control system design, including DCS, PLC and RTU configuration and programming.
  • SCADA system integration - either on-site or hosted solutions.
  • Onsite installation, commissioning and maintenance services including technical investigations and troubleshooting.
  • Communications network design.
  • SALCAN employs Registered Professional Engineers of Queensland (RPEQ) to execute design services in accordance with Queensland Professional Engineers Act.
