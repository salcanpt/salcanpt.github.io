Arrow Energy Coal Seam Gas Compressor Stations

For the Arrow Energy Compressor Stations project we provided engineering services including:

  • Hazardous Area classification and design to AS/NZS 60079
  • Safety Instrument System consultation to AS/NZS 61511 and AS/NZS 61508
  • Electrical installation verification, pre-commissioning and commissioning support
  • Control systems support across various platforms including: Schneider TSX PLC, HIMA SIS, DeltaV Process Control and SIS, Bristol Babcock RTU’s, and Honeywell Experion. 
  • Project management for the expansion and upgrade of the sites High Voltage infrastructure, including upgrade of the main 11kV trunk supply line, HV switchgear, transformers and power factor correction equipment
