Satellite Telemetry Solutions

In addition to our conventional telemetry options, SALCAN is proud to offer Satellite telemetry solutions via the Broadband Global Area Network (BGAN). This allows cost-effective control and monitoring of any process equipment, anywhere in the world. A typical scenario is Wellhead control and monitoring at remote locations. It is ideal for locations where 3G/4G is not covered and where fibre, wireless or wired networks are unavailable.

The SALCAN Satellite solution links any remote process to our SCADA network and we can provide real-time and historic data to our Clients via a customised secure mobile App.
