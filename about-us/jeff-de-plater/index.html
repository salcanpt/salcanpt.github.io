Jeff de Plater

Jeff is a licenced Electrical Contractor with Hazardous Area training and experience. Jeff has more than 24 years of experience in the Electrical, Instrumentation and Control space. During this time he has led the technical design and supervision of many industrial control system projects. Jeff works closely with customers to troubleshoot their plant issues, design solutions and deliver desired outcomes.

Jeff is a key stakeholder in SALCAN's safety management policies. He has been instrumental in implementing SALCAN's commitment to Zero Harm. SALCAN is a member of Queensland Workplace Health and Safety’ Zero Harm at Work leadership program.

Jeff also takes a leading role in SALCAN’s quality management processes through developing Inspection & Test Plan, Factory Acceptance Test, Site Acceptance Test and Check Sheet documents for SALCAN projects.

Industry experience includes:

  • Steelmaking
  • Coal Mining
  • Coal washing and handling
  • Cement plants
  • Pharmaceutical installations
  • Mine Degassing

Key Skills include: Instrumentation, LV and ELV installation and testing, panel design, PLC and RTU testing, Green and Brown field installation and commissioning, Shutdown planning and execution.
