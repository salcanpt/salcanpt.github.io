Vincent Cantwell

Vincent Cantwell is a Chartered Engineer, with an MSc in Process Automation, and over 25 years industrial experience in Process Control & Automation. Over this period he has held various senior roles with responsibilities covering the full lifecycle of industrial automation. This experience includes conceptual and detailed design, green and brown field construction projects, Facility design including Hazardous Area design, organisational development, commissioning, equipment and process validation, HAZOP’s and safety reviews, start up, process plant operation, maintenance and optimisation.

Industries worked in include;

  • Pharmaceutical & Bio-Pharmaceutical
  • Chemical;
  • Oil & Gas;
  • Food Processing

Key Skills Include; Automation, Calibration, Chemical Engineering, Conceptual Design, Electrical Infrastructure (MV / LV), Green / Brown Field Start-Ups, Facility Design, HAZOP and Safety Reviews, Instrumentation including SMART and various Bus technologies, Lean Manufacturing, Maintenance, Process Control, Project Management, Process Qualification, Six Sigma, Sustaining Operations and Optimisation, Technical Recruitment, Validation

Technology Experience Include; Alan Bradley SLC 500, Asset Management Software (AMS), Data Transmission over Radio and Ethernet, Foundation Fieldbus, Emerson Delta V, Hazardous Areas and Protection Methods, Honeywell Plantscape, Modicon TSX PLC’s, Online Process Analysers, Profibus DP, Rosemount System 3, Smart, conventional and bus based instruments from a wide variety of manufactures including ABB, Emerson, E&H, Fisher, Foxboro, Honeywell, Yokogawa.
