Process Control Equipment

SALCAN designs, constructs and installs process control equipment according to your needs.

With a focus on compliance, SALCAN employs competent engineers to provide a safe and robust solution. Products include:

  • Stand-alone Extra-Low Voltage and Low Voltage power systems according to AS/NZS4509
  • Variable Speed Drives panels
  • PLC/RTU panels using your choice of control platform
  • Pneumatic control panels
  • Custom Lithium battery and management systems
  • Portable telemetry skids
